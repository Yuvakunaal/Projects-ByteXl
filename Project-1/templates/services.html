Internal Wash

An internal car wash focuses on thoroughly cleaning the inside of a vehicle, including seats, carpets, dashboards, and other interior surfaces. This service ensures the removal of dirt, stains, and odors, leaving the car's interior looking and feeling fresh.

External Wash

An external car wash involves cleaning the vehicle's exterior surfaces, including the body, windows, wheels, and tires. This service removes dirt, grime, and contaminants, enhancing the car's appearance and protecting its paintwork.

Comprehensive Wash

A comprehensive car wash combines both internal and external cleaning services. It includes a thorough cleaning of the vehicle's exterior surfaces, such as the body, windows, and wheels, as well as a detailed cleaning of the interior, including seats, carpets, and dashboards.
